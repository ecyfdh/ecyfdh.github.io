About

Hi, I’m Elizabeth Yenzer.

I’m an animator with a particular focus on 3D animation, where I enjoy bringing ideas and characters to life in dynamic and engaging ways. My passion lies in visual storytelling, using movement, design, and expression to convey emotions and narratives that connect with people. Whether it’s crafting detailed worlds or subtle character moments, I’m driven by the challenge of making stories resonate through animation.
