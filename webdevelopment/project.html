Fountain of Dream

A short animated piece exploring dreamy lighting, particles, and mood. Built in Blender with a focus on timing and atmosphere.

Fountain of Dream

Goals: soft neon palette, reflective surfaces, subtle camera drift. I experimented with emission shaders and compositing to get the glow right without crushing detail.

  • Blender (Eevee), simple particle system for sparkles
  • Color pass in compositor; tone mapping for bloom
  • Render optimized to keep file size friendly for web

Wine Labels

Flat silhouettes only, no textures—just color, overlap, and shape language. The goal was quick shelf read with a playful, organic feel.

  • Limited palette with multiply overlaps
  • Consistent card ratios for mockups
  • Exported for print and web previews
